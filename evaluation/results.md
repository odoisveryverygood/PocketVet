# WoofFit AI Evaluation Results (CTR)

**Metric:** CTR (click-through rate) = clicks / impressions  
**Goal:** Compare a static baseline vs an adaptive personalized recommender.

## Results
- ** Baseline CTR: 0.1200
- ** Personalized CTR: 0.1025 
 

## Interpretation (Business Meaning)
If personalized CTR > baseline CTR, personalization increases engagement, which can improve retention and subscription conversion in a consumer health app.

## Notes
Synthetic data was used to isolate the causal impact of personalization vs a static rule-based approach.
